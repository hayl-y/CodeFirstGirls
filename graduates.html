Firstly we want to congratulate you for completing your degree! We at Uni Gown Ltd acknowledge the hard work that you have put in. Uni Gown Hire Ltd is the perfect place to gather your graduation essentials, and more… to help you facilitate one of the best days of your life!

Undergraduates

As an undergradute, this is most probably your first graduation! Know that we are here to support you on this graduation journey.

We will require the following measurements to ensure that it fits you.

  • circumference of your head - measure 1 inch (25mm) above your eyebrows
  • your full height (in feet and inches or centimetres)
  • If your chest is over 52" (132cm) you are further requested to supply your actual measurements
